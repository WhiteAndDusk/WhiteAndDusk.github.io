Heavy Rain ——A stroy about love.

第九艺术 互动电影 暴雨 2017/11/07

How far will you go to save someone you love?

  《Heavy Rain》is an excellent game which pruduced by Quantic Dream,one of most famous game studio among SONY.Different from most of the game studio,Quantic Dream focus in making games by the idea of film and profit from the fast development of the 3d capture technology，though games is still like a baby compared with films,we can experience a more real world in the game.

  There are four characters you need to operate in turns，making different choices will lead to the opposite results.In other word,you decision will decide who die,and who live.

  The story starts at a man who named Ethan，having a perfect family with two son and a beautiful wife.But everything changes in a afternoon that Ethan never wants to remind of it.When his wife buys cloths for another son in the mall,because of his carelessness and the corwd,his son Jayson misses!

  He tries his best to find him,but the tragedy still happens. Jayson is crashed by a car and dies .Due to the behaviour of protecting his son when the car coming,Ethan is also badly wounded…

  Ethan sleeps in the hospital for a long time and after he leaves it,everythig has changed.His wife can’t accept the truth and forgive him.The family breaks and they disvorce,they adpot the left son Shaon in turns.Ethan always in a cloud for he can’t forgive himself too,and soon he get a sickness that he always feels dizzy and appears on a strange place where rains heavily he never know…

  Meanwhile,there is a killings with unspecific targets case happens in the city.The murder will kidnap small child and put them into a well during the days having heavy rains to drown them.The most apparent feature of the case is that the murder will put a flexagon on the side of the child killed.

  The other main character who named Norman is an outstanding police working for FBI.He is appointed to investage to the case.His trump is his glasses which has the power to restore the scenes ，we call it AR now.

  The another two main characters are Scott and Madison .The former is a private detective now who changes his career as a policeman. He also seems like to find the murder.

  The later is a beautiful woman and doesn’t show her status at the first.They don’t know each other at all but the muder case put their fates together.

  The god seems to play a joke on Ethan again,he missed his son Shaon on the amusement park for his interval dizzy.When he awake on a strange place as usual,he rushes to the amusement with heavy rain,but it is too late.He calls the police,but he konws it is difficult to find a boy in such a big city.

  He is so afraid that his son is kidnapped by the murderer who has killed many small children.

  Soon the turn appears or another hell？ He finds a box when he backs to home and there are five flexagons and a phone.Ethan is required to finish five events on the flexagon and once finishes one,it will offer a part of the address where his son in.He has no choice but to do it.

Do you have the brave to save your son and put your life out of consderation? The murderer says .

  The first thing is driving on the highway by the opposite direction.It is crazy but he does it perfect with the purpose of saving his son.

Have you determined to save your son even hurting yourself?

  The second thing is cutting one of your fingers in ten minutes… In fact,I really hesitate for a while to do it.I just think about will I do it for someone I love if it is true… But I still do it and I even can feel the pain out of the display.

If you are ready to sacrifice yourself ?

  The third thing is crossing a place full of power grid straightly.Maybe it is too hard for me,I faill to do it because I only have few changes to try and it is too tired for Ethan to do it again…

Do you have the heart to kill others for your lover?

  The fourth thing is killing a person that your never meet before.When the man says don’t kill him for his cute daughter , I really make a giant determines to press the trigger…

  ”Sorry,but I have someone I have to save.”

Are you ready to kill yourself ?

  The last thing is drinking a poison in minute,after drink it,Ethan has an hour to rush to the place where his son is and save but he will die… Do you have the brave to save someone your love for killing yourself? I can’t image it and think I can’t do it forever.But I still control Ethan drink it .But for the failure of one event,I just get a part of the place where Shaon is,there are three similar places and only can choose one because of the limited time.I really hate choice!After an hesitation of five minutes,I choose a place in random.But it is a wrong place…

  Meanwhile,the other three characters also focus on the case.Madison,the beautiful woman,tring to close to Ethan to make a article because she is a juournist in fact.But she falls in love with Ethan during the time.She follows other clue and finds out the murderer…

  Actually，the murderer is Scott,the detective.He wants to revenge his father .He had a brother and a good mother in his childhood,but his father was a drinkard. On a heavy rain day,the two boys was out of the room by their drunk father.They played a hide on the rainy day and his brother was trapped by the waterpipe.Scott tried his best but he was too thin too pull his brother out.

  He returned to ask help for his father,but his father was still drunk just let him get away.So his brother died…After then,he was also sent to another family.

  So he plan the murder to test if there is a father will save his child and ignore his own life.

  How crzaying!                              ——The End

  Though the plot has some drawbacks,it is still a great game that deverse to experience!
